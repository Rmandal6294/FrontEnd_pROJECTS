Bridging Founders

Process

How It Works

A simple, transparent four-step journey from application to funding.

01
📋
Apply With Your Pitch Deck
Submit your startup details, pitch deck, and business overview through our simple online form. Takes less than 10 minutes.
02
✅
Get Shortlisted by KaroStartup
Our expert team reviews every application and selects the most promising startups for the live pitch session.
03
🎤
Pitch Live to Investors
Present your business in a curated, closed-room pitch event with active investors looking for their next big bet.
04
💰
Raise Funding & Scale
Connect with investors, close your round, and get the support to scale your business across India.
