About IDR

Where Academic
Insight Meets
Industry Reality

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute built for the frontline of digital, cyber, and technology risk. We exist to close the practitioner gap in high-consequence, regulated environments — from financial services to critical national infrastructure.

In partnership with leading UK universities, IDR combines academic depth with real-world project experience. Our learners don't just study risk — they live it, solve it, and deploy into roles that matter from day one.
